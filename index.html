As a leading infrastructure development company, we cater to a diverse range of industries, offering customized solutions that align with their specific needs. Our expertise spans across manufacturing, energy, pharmaceuticals, construction, hospitality, and commercial spaces, ensuring that each project reflects excellence in design, efficiency, and sustainability.

Our Services

At Corniche Infra Pvt. Ltd., we offer a full spectrum of services under one roof, ensuring seamless project execution and unmatched quality:

Turnkey Construction

Corniche Infracon Pvt Ltd offers comprehensive end-to-end turnkey construction solutions, delivering meticulously executed projects from initial concept to final completion.

Architecture & Interior Design

At Corniche Infracon Pvt Ltd, we maintain an unwavering focus on sustainable and innovative designs that simultaneously enhance both aesthetic appeal and practical usability.

Civil Construction Works

Our construction division is equipped with cutting-edge technology and advanced machinery, complemented by a highly skilled workforce trained in the latest construction methodologies.

Electrical & Plumbing Services

Corniche Infracon Pvt Ltd offers comprehensive electrical contracting services that cater to diverse project requirements across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

POP Ceiling Work & Kitchen Design

At Corniche Infracon Pvt Ltd, our POP (Plaster of Paris) ceiling work design services represent the perfect fusion of artistic creativity and technical expertise and lighting-integrated ceiling solutions

IT & Home Automation Systems

At Corniche Infracon Pvt Ltd, we integrate cutting-edge IT and home automation systems to enhance modern living and working environments, creating spaces that are not just smart but intuitively responsive

Lift Installation & HVAC Systems

Corniche Infracon Pvt Ltd offers comprehensive lift installation and AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) services, ensuring seamless vertical mobility and safety in residential, commercial, and industrial spaces.

Networking & CCTV Solutions

Corniche Infracon Pvt Ltd offers comprehensive Networking and services include the design, installation, and maintenance of advanced networking systems and state-of-the-art CCTV surveillance setups.
